BarCeloneta Sangria Bar

Calle Sevilla 70, Barcelona, Spain, 08003

Bar and restaurant by the beach offering organic vegan food with tapas, burgers, raw food and vegan cheese. Thursday is paella day. Specializes in sangria (with and without alcohol) and has organic wine and beer. The raw desserts are sugar-free and gluten-free. Open Mon 7:00pm-11:30pm, Wed-Sun 12:00pm-4:00pm, Wed-Sun 7:00pm-11:30pm. Closed for holiday April 24 - 30.

Category: Vegan, Italian, Organic, Mediterranean, Beer/Wine, Spanish
